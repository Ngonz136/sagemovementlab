Personal Training 101

Training with SageMovementLab is simple, personal, and built to last.

My Approach

Every plan starts with your goals, schedule, and training history. From there, we create a realistic routine you can stick with — combining technique, progression, and accountability. I’m a NASM-certified personal trainer and a black belt in martial arts; I coach the whole person: mind, movement, and method.

The Three Pillars

Mind

Simple cues, clear goals, and consistent habits — so progress stays steady and stress stays low.

Movement

Foundations first: quality reps, safe technique, and scalable progressions you can feel.

Method

Evidence-based programming with check-ins, visual feedback, and adjustments as you grow.

What to Expect in a Session

  • Brief check-in: sleep, energy, any aches
  • Warm-up for mobility + activation
  • Main lifts or circuits tailored to your focus
  • Finisher (conditioning or core) as needed
  • Cool-down + notes for next time

How It Works

  1. Free Consult: goals, schedule, preferences
  2. Assessment: movement screen + baseline metrics
  3. Training Plan: customized split + progressions
  4. Check-Ins: quick reviews with measurable wins
