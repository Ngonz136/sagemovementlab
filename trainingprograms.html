Training Programs

Choose a program to match your goals, then book a consult to personalize your plan.

Weight Loss

A sustainable approach focused on calorie awareness, strength circuits, and NEAT (daily movement).
Expect progressive training, realistic nutrition habits, and weekly check-ins
that keep momentum without burnout.

  • 3–4 day split • 45–60 min
  • Strength + interval finishers
  • Simple nutrition targets & habit tracking

Cardiovascular Endurance

Build a stronger engine with steady-state cardio, intervals, and cross-training.
We’ll progress time, pace, and recovery to boost stamina, heart health, and
efficiency—without pounding your joints.

  • 2–4 cardio sessions/week
  • Zone training + intervals
  • Mobility & core for longevity

Strength

Get stronger with compound lifts and smart progression. We’ll dial in technique,
tempo, and volume to build power, muscle, and confidence—on and off the platform.

  • 3–5 day split • 45–75 min
  • Progressive overload & form coaching
  • Optional athletic/striking accessories
