About Coach Nat

I’m Coach Nat, founder of SageMovementLab. I’m a NASM-certified personal trainer and a black belt in martial arts with over a decade of coaching and leadership experience. Through my own fitness journey, I discovered the transformative power of movement and the deep connection between mind and body.
I help people move with confidence, sharpen their focus, and feel empowered from the inside out. My mission is to make fitness practical, mindful, and built to last, cultivating strength that extends beyond the gym.
